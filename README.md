# BudgetWise-AI-s-Budget-Convergence-Score
The coinage of the term "Budget Convergence Score" for personal finance applications.

 * BUDGET CONVERGENCE SCORE (BCS)
 * coined by Joseph Robinson · BudgetWise AI · June 2026
 * ============================================================
 *
 * The Budget Convergence Score measures how rapidly and consistently
 * a person's spending is CONVERGING toward their ideal budget —
 * not just whether they hit their budget this month, but whether
 * their trajectory is improving over time.
 *
 * Most tools ask: "Did you hit your budget?"
 * BCS asks:       "Are you getting better?"
 *
 * Formula:
 *   BCS = (0.35 × CAS) + (0.30 × TS) + (0.20 × SES) + (0.15 × SR)
 *
 *   CAS  – Category Accuracy Score   (actual vs. budgeted per category)
 *   TS   – Trajectory Score          (month-over-month improvement)
 *   SES  – Subscription Efficiency   (active use of paid subscriptions)
 *   SR   – Savings Rate Score        (actual vs. target savings rate)
 *
 * BCS Bands:
 *   85–100  Converging   – strong trajectory toward goals
 *   70–84   Aligning     – positive trend, minor gaps
 *   55–69   Stabilizing  – mixed, no clear regression
 *   40–54   Drifting     – stagnant or slow regression
 *   0–39    Diverging    – spending moving away from goals
